About Me

I'm a student at NAISHK and a virtuoso pianist with accolades from HKPPA, JP PIARA, and HKYPAF. Also known as Ryan WG. Leung / Ryan L, I've earned Academic Awards in both Year 3 and Year 6, and a Music Award in Year 4. Beyond academic life, I lead Smart Study (my startup that focuses on providing the right learning tools like flashcards) and co-lead Free-Fall News (a student-run news outlet).
